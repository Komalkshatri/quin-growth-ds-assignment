# quin-growth-ds-assignment
This repo contains my submission for Quin Growth Data Science role's assessment.

## Contents
`Quin_Assessment.ipynb` — analysis notebook containing funnel analysis, retention analysis, experiment design calculations, and predictive modelling workflow
`Quin_Assignment.pdf` — written summary, thought process, analysis and business recommendations for given Tasks 1–5

## Summary of Findings
Key findings from the analysis include:

- The largest onboarding drop-off occurred during device pairing
- Organic and referral cohorts showed stronger 30-day retention than paid social cohorts
- Users who completed ride-related behaviors early showed substantially stronger long-term retention outcomes
- Onboarding simplification and lifecycle interventions may improve activation and retention outcomes
- A meaningful portion of premium trial users became inactive before conversion, suggesting an opportunity for targeted lifecycle re-engagement at early stages
